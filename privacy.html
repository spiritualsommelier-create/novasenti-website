2.3 Client operational data — our role as processor

When clients upload operational data to NovaSenti (weekly structured data files), we process that data as a data processor on the client's behalf. The client is the data controller for that data. Our processing is governed by the DPA, not this policy.

Acquisition data (standard): aggregated, source-level data containing cohort-level metrics only. No individual player or customer identifiers are included. This data does not typically constitute Personal Data.

CRM data (optional, separately activated): anonymised individual-level data (e.g. per-player activity records with hashed identifiers). Where CRM data is elected, clients are responsible for anonymising the data before upload. Processing is governed by the DPA and any applicable DPA addendum.

Benchmarking: we use aggregated, anonymised client data to develop cross-client market benchmarks. Once anonymised, this data is no longer Personal Data and we retain and use it as an independent controller for the purpose of improving and delivering benchmark outputs. See the DPA for the anonymisation standard applied.

3. How we use personal data

We use personal data only for the purposes for which it was collected (as described in section 2 above) and for the following compatible purposes:

  • Delivering, maintaining, and improving the NovaSenti platform and modules
  • Communicating with clients and prospective clients about the platform, their account, and support queries
  • Meeting our legal and regulatory obligations (including GDPR compliance)
  • Protecting the security and integrity of the platform

We do not use personal data for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects, and we do not use personal data for advertising or marketing profiling.

6. Your rights under GDPR

You have the following rights in respect of personal data we hold about you as a controller:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: ask us to delete your personal data where there is no overriding legal basis for continued processing.
  • Restriction: ask us to restrict processing while a dispute is resolved.
  • Objection: object to processing based on legitimate interests. We will cease processing unless we can demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds.
  • Portability: receive your data in a machine-readable format where processing is based on consent or contract and carried out automatically.
  • Withdraw consent: where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing.

To exercise any right, contact us at help@novasenti.com. We will respond within 30 days (extendable by 2 months for complex requests with notice). You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Malta Information and Data Protection Commissioner (IDPC) at idpc.org.mt, or with your local supervisory authority if you are based in another EU member state.

Note on client operational data: where personal data forms part of data uploaded by a client to the platform, we process it as a data processor on the client's behalf. Data subject rights requests relating to such data should be directed to the client (the data controller), who will engage us as necessary under the DPA.

7. Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies only to operate the platform. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies. No consent banner is displayed because consent is not required for strictly necessary cookies. If we introduce any non-essential cookies in future, we will update our Cookie Policy and obtain consent before setting them. See our Cookie Policy for full detail.

8. Security

We maintain appropriate technical and organisational security measures including TLS encryption in transit, authenticated access, server-side tenant isolation, access logging, and regular security reviews. We will notify affected clients and the relevant supervisory authority of any personal data breach in accordance with our GDPR obligations.

9. Children

The NovaSenti platform is a B2B service intended for business users only. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 18. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to this policy

We will update this policy when our data practices change. The current version and date are shown at the top. Material changes will be notified to registered clients by email at least 14 days before they take effect. The most recent version is always available at novasenti.com.
