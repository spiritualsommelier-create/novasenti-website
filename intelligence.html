Module 4

CRM Benchmarks

Add-On · Standalone No Data Supply Required No Module 1 or 2 Required

Your engagement metrics look strong. But strong compared to what?

Know whether your CRM is genuinely outperforming the market. Benchmark conversion, onboarding, early life engagement, retention and ARPU across industry, geo and vertical to see exactly where you stand.

  • Are your 2nd deposit or further engagement rates competitive? Benchmark early life engagement against businesses in the same vertical and geo. Know whether onboarding drop-off is a product problem, a CRM problem or simply the market norm
  • Entering a new market? Understand what conversion, early life engagement and onboarding retention rates look like in your target vertical and geo before you commit. If the market benchmark shows strong early life drop-off or high incentive dependency across the vertical, your CRM model needs to account for that from day one
  • Benchmark CRM spend as a percentage of gross revenue against your market and vertical. Know whether your incentive model is driving sustainable engagement or masking the true cost of retention before it shows up in net revenue
  • Are high value users being retained and engaged at a rate that justifies their acquisition and ongoing CRM cost? Benchmark high value user engagement, churn rate and ARPU by lifecycle stage against comparable businesses to understand whether your product and CRM model are delivering the value they should
  • Reactivation should recover revenue not just volume. Benchmark reactivation rates and post-reactivation ARPU against your market. If reactivation spend is rising while ARPU of reactivated cohorts falls, you have a value problem not a volume problem
  • What percentage of users convert to multi-product engagement in your market and vertical? Cross-sell conversion benchmarks by lifecycle stage reveal whether your CRM is capturing available revenue from your existing base or leaving it behind
  • Access requires no subscription to Module 1 or 2. No data supply is required
