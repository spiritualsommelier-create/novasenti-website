NovaSenti · Nova Prospera Ltd · Version 1.0

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, remember your preferences, and provide information to site owners.

What cookies does NovaSenti use?

We use strictly necessary cookies only. These are cookies that are essential for the platform to function — without them, services you have asked for (like staying logged in) cannot be provided. Under GDPR and the ePrivacy Directive, strictly necessary cookies do not require your consent.

We do not currently use:

  • Analytics or performance cookies (e.g. Google Analytics)
  • Marketing or advertising cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Any third-party cookies that track you across websites

Do I need to accept cookies?

Because we use strictly necessary cookies only, we do not display a cookie consent banner — consent is not required for these cookies. If we introduce any non-essential cookies in the future, we will update this policy, add a consent banner, and not set those cookies until you have actively accepted them.

How to control cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling strictly necessary cookies will prevent the platform from working correctly — you will not be able to stay logged in.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Changes to this policy

If we introduce new cookies — particularly any non-essential cookies — we will update this policy and, where required, obtain your consent before setting them.
